Pargas Gaming Community

Pargas Gaming Community rf (inofficially PGC) is an association founded 2014 with the goals to promote and develope the interest for information and communication technology as a hobby and freetime occupation. The association arranges and supports LAN events as well as other forms of IT and computer games related leisure activities. PGC as an association has grown a lot in just a few years and we would like to become even more involved with the youth. We noticed that there is interest in the e-sports scene and therefore PGC has started hosting leagues within competitive games like Counter Strike: Global Offensive and Hearthstone.

PGC League

PGCL is a Counter Strike: Global Offensive league that is open for everyone.

The league is divided into two groups; premier and amateur. Premier league was made for those who want to play more competitively. Amateur league is free and anyone can participate.

Premier League

  • 16 teams
  • Every thursday 19:30 Eastern European Time
  • Matches are streamed on Twitch
  • Entry fee 50e per team (everything goes towards the prize)
  • Finals are played offline

Amateur League

  • Unlimited teams
  • Every thursday 19:30 Eastern European Time
  • Is not streamed on Twitch
  • No entry fee
  • Finals are played online
